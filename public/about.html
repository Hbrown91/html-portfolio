About Me



My name is Helen, though most people know me as Lisi. I’m currently transitioning into web development, building on a long-standing curiosity about how things work behind the scenes.

My interest in coding started when I was introduced to HTML basics at eight years old. Even though life eventually pulled me in other directions, including creative writing and storytelling, I never forgot being a kid that was handed a book that was almost too big for me to carry: HTML for Dummies.

Over the years, I’ve explored poetry, written fiction, and developed a strong interest in narrative design and worldbuilding. More recently, I’ve returned to programming with renewed focus, starting with HTML and continuing into CSS and JavaScript.

I’m currently working through a full-stack web development program and building projects that strengthen both my technical skills and problem-solving approach. I enjoy learning by doing, and I’m excited to keep growing in this field.
